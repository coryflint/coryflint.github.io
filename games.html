Red Hearts

Explore tiny dungeons in a game full of heart! Battle monsters, dodge traps, and throw shurikens in quick bursts of action. Red Hearts is currently in development by two brothers with lots more in the works: new enemies, dungeons, and secrets to discover. If you love it or have feedback, please let us know. 2016

Tiny Armies

Epic battles of tiny proportions! Tiny Armies is a fast-paced game of conquest. Simply swipe your units to conquer the enemy and strategize as you encounter lakes, mountains, and forests along the way. Quick bursts of gameplay with a striking presentation, designed for iPhone, Apple Watch, and iMessage.

Featured as a Quick-Fix Game on the App Store

2016

Coffee Pot Terrarium

Created by two brothers, Coffee Pot Terrarium is a turn-based puzzle game of tile manipulation. Raise new pathways to traverse the map, drop tiles to eliminate the enemy. Stop and smell the cherry blossoms.

Google Play Indie Games Festival 2016 Finalist
Featured in the Indie Corner Collection on the Google Play Store
