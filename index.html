Hi, I'm Cory Flint!

Designer and illustrator for Brothers Flint.

I'm a 26-year-old game designer and artist with a passion for innovative game mechanics, artisan graphics, and design principles. My brother Toby and I joined forces eight years ago in college to create the Brothers Flint mobile app and game development studio. Our games have been featured on both the App Store and Google Play Store, and one was a Finalist in the first-ever Google Play Indie Games Festival 2016.
