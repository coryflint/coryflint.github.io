Hi, I'm Cory Flint!

Designer and illustrator for Brothers Flint.

I'm a designer who likes to approach design projects as puzzles to be solved. My brother Toby and I joined forces eight years ago in college to create the Brothers Flint mobile app and game development studio. We've developed apps for clients across the US and our games have been featured on both the App Store and Google Play Store.
