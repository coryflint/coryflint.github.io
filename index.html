Hi, I'm Cory Flint!

Designer and illustrator for Brothers Flint.

Seven years ago, my brother and I decided to make a game together during our freshman year of college. We had such a good time that we went into business as the Brothers Flint. Our games have been featured on both the App Store and Play Store, and one was a finalist in the first-ever Google Play Indie Games Festival 2016. 2017

Red Hearts

Explore tiny dungeons in a game full of heart! Battle monsters, dodge traps, and throw shurikens in quick bursts of action. Red Hearts is currently in development by two brothers with lots more in the works: new enemies, dungeons, and secrets to discover. If you love it or have feedback, please let us know. Thanks for playing!

2016

Tiny Armies

Tiny Armies is a fast-paced game of conquest. Simply swipe your units to conquer the enemy and strategize as you encounter lakes, mountains, and forests along the way. Quick bursts of gameplay with a striking presentation, designed for your Apple Watch.

Featured as a Quick-Fix Game on the App Store

2016

Coffee Pot Terrarium

Created by two brothers, Coffee Pot Terrarium is a turn-based puzzle game of tile manipulation. Raise new pathways to traverse the map, drop tiles to eliminate the enemy. Stop and smell the cherry blossoms.

Google Play Indie Games Festival 2016 Finalist
Featured in the Indie Corner Collection on the Google Play Store

2015

Gallop through the vintage pixelated Spanish countryside as you tilt at windmills upon the mighty back of Rocinante! Or a motorcycle! Or a reindeer! Or Sancho Panza! Unlock these and other ridiculous mounts in an endless runner that's spinning around in developer's hell until further notice.

Satellite Defense

Build satellites, blast missiles, and protect planets in a game that's out of this world! Do you have what it takes to defend the solar system? Satellite Defense was designed by two brothers in the basement of a college dorm over the course of their freshman year.
